About Us

Paysys Labs is your trusted source in Fintech services and support

Paysys Labs is a leading provider of digital financial services, established in 2016. Over the past years, we have grown to become a trusted partner in the fintech industry, delivering innovative and turnkey solutions to clients across various sectors. Our robust and scalable platforms enable seamless digital interactions for millions of customers, enhancing their access to financial products and services. With expertise in instant payment systems, card management, and mobile banking, Paysys Labs is committed to driving digital transformation and fostering financial inclusion. Our dedicated team of industry experts continuously strives to provide secure, efficient, and cutting-edge financial technology solutions that meet the evolving needs of our clients and their customers.

Our Products

Managed digital financial solutions & services customized for your industry

Open Connect

OpenConnect is a scalable, cloud-ready middleware platform designed for integrating payment systems, core banking, wallet platforms, and digital channels.

Open CMS

OpenCMS is a Card Management System with an integrated CRM solution that enables customers to manage the issuance of debit and prepaid cards

Open ACS

OpenACS is a secure, scalable Access Control Server for managing 3D Secure transactions, supporting 3DS 1.0 and 2.0, with easy integration into payment systems.

Open Wallet

Open Wallet is an omni-channel solution for hosting both consumer and agent based mobile money accounts

Open Digital

Open Digital is a platform that enables banks, telcos, and microfinance institutions to offer mobile banking access to their customers anytime, anywhere.

Open Acquiring

Open Acquiring efficiently manages payments, offering secure integrations and high transaction support.
